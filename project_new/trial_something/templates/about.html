Who are we?

We are McGill students and graduates based in Montreal, and we were fellows at the AI4Good Lab. This website, unbIAased, was our final project at the AI4Good Lab, where we focused on using machine learning for social good.

As participants in the AI4Good Lab summer program, our mission is to develop machine learning-based solutions to real-world problems while adhering to ethical design principles. The medical field is complex and constantly evolving, and we aim to create a tool that helps doctors and patients understand the risks of taking certain medications. Not all drugs are tested equally, especially for female patients. Our tool predicts the risks of taking specific medications based on the patient's sex and age, providing valuable insights to support informed decision-making.
